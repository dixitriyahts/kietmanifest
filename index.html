Introduction

Manifest 2024, the annual management fest of KIET School of Management. Manifest has been an integral part of our institution's calendar, providing a platform for students to showcase their managerial skills, network with industry experts, and foster an environment of learning beyond the classroom.

Objectives of Manifest

The primary objectives of Manifest 2024 are as follows:

  • Create a practical learning environment that supplements theoretical knowledge by organizing competitive events, workshops, and seminars.

  • Bridge the gap between academia and industry by inviting seasoned professionals to share insights, mentor participants, and provide career guidance.

  • Offer a diverse range of events encompassing various domains such as marketing, finance, human resources, operations, and entrepreneurship, nurturing multifaceted skill sets among participants.

  • Encourage teamwork, leadership, and innovation through collaborative events that challenge students' problem-solving abilities.

Activities and Events

Manifest 2024 aims to offer an engaging line-up of activities and events spread over three days, including:

  • Case study competitions.
  • Panel discussions on emerging trends in management.
  • Workshops on leadership development, digital marketing, financial modelling, and more.
  • Cultural evenings showcasing talents beyond academics.
  • Management games and activities.
  • Management club activities.
  • Management Treasure hunt and auction activities.
  • Management shayri to lighten the mood everyone present around.
  • Management singing stall to dedicate songs to anyone by anyone.
  • Different food stalls to eat and enjoy the event.
  • Influencers to grace the event and increase the footfall.
  • Registration desks in college 3 days prior to the event so that other departments can also participate in every event.
  • A management start-up Stall which will be SPOC for every query related to start-up businesses and information related to it. It will provide a platform for those who want to get an idea about industry, policies, schemes, government norms and finance sources.
